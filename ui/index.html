Killer Whale

The Killer whale's large size and strength make it among the fastest marine mammals,able to reach speeds in excess of 30 knots(about 34 mph)

  • Scientific Name: Orcinus orca
  • Average Length: 7 to 9.7 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 50 to 80 years
  • Habitat: Marine

Orcas, or killer whales, are the largest of the dolphins and one of the world's most powerful predators. They feast on marine mammals such as seals, sea lions, and even whales, employing teeth that can be four inches (ten centimeters) long.They use echolocation to communicate and hunt, making sounds that travel underwater until they encounter objects, then bounce back, revealing their location, size, and shape.
